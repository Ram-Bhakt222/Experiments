## The magic moment

Same face. Same outfit. Same background. Twelve haircuts and a color season in 60 seconds. The face-preserving image edit IS the scroll-stopper. Every hook, ad, and influencer brief centers on it.

## The 5-step reveal loop (used in every video)

1. Establishing shot — creator in current hair (0–3 s)
2. Setup line — "I let AI tell me every haircut I'd actually look good in" (3–6 s)
3. Grid reveal — 4–8 styles flash by on the same face (6–11 s)
4. The "wait, this one" pause on the surprise winner (11–14 s)
5. CTA — link in bio / comment HAIR (14–15 s)

## Tier-1 hooks (10)

POV · reverse reveal · stylist roulette · color-season exposé · "the avoid list" · decade test · mom/daughter or siblings · hair-loss POV · salt-and-pepper · bride.

## Micro-influencer playbook

- 50 creators at 1K–10K followers across 8 niche pools (curly + texture, salt-and-pepper, hair-loss, bridal, men's barbershop, mid-life reinvention, kids' hair, color-season).
- Recruit with the "paid promo?" DM line (inbox-preview-optimized).
- Pay bundles ($400–$800 for 4 videos), +$200 bonus past 100K views.
- No exclusivity in cohort 1.

## Channel allocation

- **Primary:** TikTok (daily), IG Reels, Pinterest (under-allocated by category)
- **Paid:** Meta Ads (30 creatives across 6 personas, creative-as-targeting)
- **Secondary:** YouTube Shorts, founder LinkedIn (B2B side-door to salons)

## Hard anti-patterns

- Fake AI-avatar testimonials (kills trust faster than it scales)
- Filters on faces in marketing (undercuts the face-preservation proof)
- Scalp / hair-loss diagnosis claims (FDA / medical-device territory)
- Single-ethnicity, single-texture casting (HALO must visibly work on 4c/4b, gray, alopecia from round one)
- Spending on paid before organic proves the hook

## 90-day targets

50K+ installs · 10M+ cumulative TikTok views · top organic video 1M+ · CAC < $6 · free → paid 4–7% · referral install share > 15% · 50-creator cohort · 3 tech + 5 beauty press hits.
